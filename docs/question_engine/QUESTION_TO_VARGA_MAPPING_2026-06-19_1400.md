## SECTION 1: ASTROLOGICAL PREDICTION PRINCIPLES

To provide accurate and deterministic astrological predictions, the Question Engine relies on a multi-layered evaluation framework:

*   **Promise:** The foundational potential or karma for an event to occur, determined primarily by the D1 (Rasi) chart.
*   **Strength:** The quantitative capacity of the planets and houses involved, measured via Shadbala and Bhava Bala.
*   **Timing:** The chronological activation of the promise, governed by the Dasha system (and future transits).
*   **Confidence:** The certainty of the prediction, validated by checking corresponding Varga (divisional) charts.
*   **Risk:** The mitigating or destructive factors that can delay or deny the promise, evaluated through Doshas and malefic influences.

### Role of Astrological Elements
*   **Bhavas (Houses):** Define the areas of life (e.g., 7th for Marriage, 10th for Career).
*   **Planets (Karakas & Lords):** Act as the agents executing the karma.
*   **Yogas:** Specific planetary combinations that elevate or suppress the baseline promise.
*   **Doshas:** Flaws or blemishes in the chart that create friction, delays, or obstacles.
*   **Vargas (Divisional Charts):** Microscopic views of specific life domains used to confirm the D1 promise.
*   **Dashas (Planetary Periods):** The timeline mechanism that activates specific planets and houses.

## SECTION 3: DOSHA GOVERNANCE

The system strictly avoids binary (Yes/No) Dosha evaluations. All Doshas are calculated using a Severity Model that accounts for cancellations and mitigating factors.

**Model Structure:**
*   **Severity Score (0-100):** Raw strength of the malefic formation.
*   **Cancellation Score (0-100):** Strength of benefic aspects, exaltations, or specific Yoga भंग (cancellation) rules.
*   **Net Impact Score:** `Severity Score - Cancellation Score` (Floor 0).
*   **Affected Domains:** Which Question Domains this Dosha penalizes.

### Kuja Dosha
*   **Severity Score:** Based on Mars placement in 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 12 from Lagna, Moon, or Venus.
*   **Cancellation Score:** Mars in own sign, exalted, aspected by Jupiter, or specific degree exemptions.
*   **Net Impact Score:** Calculated difference.
*   **Affected Domains:** Marriage, Health, Property.

### Kalasarpa Dosha
*   **Severity Score:** All planets hemmed between Rahu and Ketu.
*   **Cancellation Score:** Presence of strong benefic outside the axis, or Raja Yogas.
*   **Net Impact Score:** Calculated difference.
*   **Affected Domains:** Master Life Analysis, Career, Wealth.

### Guru Chandal
*   **Severity Score:** Conjunction or mutual aspect of Jupiter and Rahu/Ketu.
*   **Cancellation Score:** High Shadbala of Jupiter, benefic aspects.
*   **Net Impact Score:** Calculated difference.
*   **Affected Domains:** Spirituality, Education, Wealth.

### Papakartari
*   **Severity Score:** A house or planet hemmed between two malefics.
*   **Cancellation Score:** Benefic aspecting the hemmed planet/house.
*   **Net Impact Score:** Calculated difference.
*   **Affected Domains:** Variable (Depends on the house/planet hemmed).

### Pitru Dosha
*   **Severity Score:** Afflictions to the 9th house, 9th lord, or Sun by Rahu/Saturn.
*   **Cancellation Score:** Strong Jupiter aspect on the 9th house/Sun.
*   **Net Impact Score:** Calculated difference.
*   **Affected Domains:** Master Life Analysis, Wealth, Progeny.

## SECTION 4: QUESTION SCORING MODEL

Every prediction generated by the engine is backed by a mathematical formula:

**Prediction Score = Promise × Activation × Confidence × Modifier**

*   **Promise (0.0 - 1.0):** The baseline potential derived from the D1 chart (Bhava Bala + Lord Shadbala + Karaka Strength).
*   **Activation (0.0 - 1.0):** The relevance of the current/target Dasha periods to the active domain.
*   **Confidence (0.5 - 1.5):** The multiplier derived from validating the Varga charts (Section 5).
*   **Modifier (0.0 - 1.0):** The penalty applied by the Net Impact Score of active Doshas (1.0 = No Dosha, 0.0 = Absolute block).

---

## SECTION 5: CONFIDENCE SCORE MODEL

The Confidence multiplier is determined by the alignment between the D1 chart and the domain-specific Vargas.

*   **Supportive Vargas:** If the relevant Varga (e.g., D9 for Marriage) confirms the dignity of the D1 Karaka (e.g., Venus is exalted or in own sign in D9), the Confidence multiplier increases.
*   **Conflicting Vargas:** If the Varga contradicts the D1 (e.g., Venus is debilitated in D9 while strong in D1), the Confidence multiplier decreases, indicating surface-level promise but underlying weakness.
*   **The Role of D60 (Shashtiamsha):** D60 is the ultimate tie-breaker and karmic root checker. It is applied as a final weight to the Confidence score. A strong D60 placement can override a weak secondary Varga.

## SECTION 6: TIMING GOVERNANCE

The chronological mapping of when a predicted event will occur.

### Current MVP
*   **MD (Mahadasha):** Defines the broad 7-20 year epoch.
*   **AD (Antardasha):** Defines the 1-3 year window of manifestation.
*   **PD (Pratyantardasha):** Defines the micro-window (weeks/months) for exact triggering.

### Future Integrations
*   **Gochara:** Will act as an intersecting boolean filter. If MD/AD/PD agree, the event triggers *only when* Jupiter/Saturn transit favorably.
*   **Ashtakavarga:** Transit validation based on SAV/BAV points in the transiting sign.
*   **Mandali Transit Model:** Proprietary Moon-centric transit framework for precise emotional/event triggers.
