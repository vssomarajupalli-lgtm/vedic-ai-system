# MASTER LIFE ANALYSIS QUESTIONS

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Version:** Phase 9 Step 3A Governance Package

This document catalogs 25-30 curated, high-level Master Life Analysis queries. These questions require cross-Bhava synthesis and cannot be answered by looking at a single house.

## Core Identity & Karma
1. What are my greatest strengths?
2. What are my greatest weaknesses?
3. What are my karmic challenges?
4. What is my overarching life purpose (Dharma)?
5. What are my top success areas in this lifetime?
6. What are my primary risk areas or vulnerabilities?
7. How does my past karma influence my present life?
8. What spiritual path or practice best suits me?

## Career & Wealth
9. Which profession or career path suits me best?
10. Which profession or industry should I avoid?
11. What is the best way for me to earn money?
12. Will I be more successful in business or in a structured job?
13. Am I destined for sudden wealth or steady, gradual accumulation?
14. How should I manage my resources for maximum stability?

## Relationships & Family
15. What type of partner will bring me the most stability and growth?
16. What are the key lessons I must learn in intimate relationships?
17. How will my relationship with my immediate family shape my life?
18. Will my primary focus be on domestic life or public life?

## Health & Vitality
19. What is my natural physical constitution and vitality level?
20. What chronic lifestyle adjustments should I make for long-term health?
21. At what periods in my life must I be most cautious about my well-being?

## Growth & Environment
22. Will I thrive better in my homeland or in a foreign country?
23. What types of environments (urban, nature, corporate, creative) bring out my best?
24. How do I best handle periods of intense obstacle or crisis?
25. What is the most important lesson I am meant to learn in this incarnation?
