# QUESTION LIBRARY MASTER

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Version:** Phase 9 Step 3A Governance Package

## Structure

The Master Question Library catalogs 100-150 curated questions that the system is natively trained to answer with high confidence and deep astrological formulas.

The library is structured into three distinct categories:

### A. Master Life Analysis
Holistic, high-level queries that scan the entire chart to synthesize overarching life themes.
*Examples: Life purpose, karmic challenges, top strengths.*
**(See: [MASTER_LIFE_ANALYSIS_QUESTIONS](MASTER_LIFE_ANALYSIS_QUESTIONS_2026-06-19_1130.md))**

### B. Bhava Questions
Questions strictly routed to specific astrological houses (Bhavas).

* **1st House (Self):** Health, constitution, general life path, personality.
* **2nd House (Wealth/Family):** Savings, immediate family, speech.
* **3rd House (Courage/Siblings):** Short journeys, siblings, self-effort, communication.
* **4th House (Home/Mother):** Real estate, peace of mind, mother, early education.
* **5th House (Children/Intellect):** Progeny, creativity, investments, romance.
* **6th House (Enemies/Disease):** Debts, daily work, conflicts, acute illnesses.
* **7th House (Marriage/Partnerships):** Spouse, business partners, public dealings.
* **8th House (Longevity/Obstacles):** Inheritances, sudden changes, occult, longevity.
* **9th House (Dharma/Fortune):** Luck, religion, higher education, father.
* **10th House (Career/Status):** Profession, fame, authority, public reputation.
* **11th House (Gains/Network):** Large sums of money, elder siblings, social circles.
* **12th House (Losses/Spirituality):** Foreign travel, expenditures, enlightenment, isolation.

### C. Custom Questions
An infinite space of user-defined queries. The Question Router parses these into components (Domain, Subject, Timeframe) and dynamically constructs a formula path using the Formula Repository if a predefined Bhava Question does not exist.
