## Standardized Output Contract

To ensure every response generated by the Vedic AI System is comprehensive, logical, and structured, the Answer Composer enforces a strict 5-part contract. The Final Answer object must contain the following components regardless of the query domain.

### 1. Promise (Natal Potential)
What does the birth chart promise regarding this domain?
*Example: "Your chart shows a strong promise for wealth accumulation..."*

### 2. Strength (Quantitative Evaluation)
A clear, quantitative statement of the strength of the astrological indicators.
*Example: "...because your 2nd House Lord (Jupiter) has a high Shadbala of 145% and the Bhava Bala is exceptionally strong."*

### 3. Reason (Astrological Logic)
The exact astrological reasoning behind the promise, expressed in accessible terms.
*Example: "Jupiter is seated in its own sign in the 2nd house and receives a benefic aspect from Venus, creating a powerful Dhana Yoga."*

### 4. Timing (Manifestation Window)
When this promise is likely to activate or manifest in the user's life, determined by the Timing Engine.
*Example: "This wealth potential is highly likely to activate during your upcoming Jupiter Mahadasha, specifically between 2028 and 2030."*

### 5. Advice (Remedial & Practical Guidance)
Actionable advice, warnings, or astrological remedies.
*Example: "To maximize this period, avoid speculative investments and focus on long-term assets. Chanting the Jupiter mantra on Thursdays will further enhance these results."*
