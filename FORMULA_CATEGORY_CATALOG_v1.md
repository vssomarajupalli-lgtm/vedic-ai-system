# FORMULA CATEGORY CATALOG v1

## 1. Overview
This catalog establishes the 10 structural Master Categories for the Formula Repository. These categories dictate the astrological boundaries and default engine requirements for all underlying formulas.

---

## 2. Master Categories

### 2.1 Marriage & Relationships
- **Focus**: Partnerships, legal unions, matrimonial delays, and relationship quality.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `DashaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D9 (Navamsha).
- **Core Karakas (Significators)**: Venus, Jupiter, 7th House, 7th Lord.

### 2.2 Career & Profession
- **Focus**: Job acquisition, career growth, promotions, workplace changes, and professional prestige.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `TransitEngine`, `DashaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D10 (Dasamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Sun, Mercury, Saturn, 10th House, 10th Lord.

### 2.3 Wealth & Finance
- **Focus**: Income, savings potential, sudden gains, financial losses, and asset accumulation.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `AshtakavargaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1.
- **Core Karakas**: Jupiter, Venus, 2nd House, 11th House.

### 2.4 Education & Learning
- **Focus**: Academic success, higher education, competitive exams, and intelligence.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `DashaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D24 (Chaturvimshamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Jupiter, Mercury, 4th House, 5th House, 9th House.

### 2.5 Children & Progeny
- **Focus**: Childbirth, relationship with children, and creative pursuits.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `TransitEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D7 (Saptamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Jupiter, 5th House, 5th Lord.

### 2.6 Property & Vehicles
- **Focus**: Real estate acquisition, vehicle purchases, and comforts.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D4 (Chaturthamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Mars (Property), Venus (Vehicles), 4th House.

### 2.7 Health & Wellness
- **Focus**: Physical vitality, disease recovery, and medical vulnerabilities.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `DashaEngine`, `TransitEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D3 (Drekkana), D6 (Shashthamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Sun, Lagna, 6th House, 8th House.

### 2.8 Foreign Settlement & Travel
- **Focus**: Immigration, long-distance travel, and success in foreign lands.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `DashaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D9, D12.
- **Core Karakas**: Rahu, Moon, 9th House, 12th House.

### 2.9 Spirituality & Occult
- **Focus**: Spiritual growth, intuition, hidden knowledge, and liberation.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `NatalPromiseEngine`, `YogaEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1, D20 (Vimshamsha).
- **Core Karakas**: Ketu, Jupiter, 8th House, 9th House, 12th House.

### 2.10 General Life Progress
- **Focus**: Broad life trends, immediate dasha analysis, and overarching destiny.
- **Primary Engines Required**: `DashaEngine`, `TransitEngine`.
- **Primary Vargas Required**: D1.
- **Core Karakas**: Lagna, Lagnesh, Moon, Sun.
